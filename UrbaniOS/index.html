Vegan Slippers For the Win

I love the look of the Emu Australia Mayberry Slippers, but I recently have gone vegan and that extend to everything in my life. As you know Emu uses sheepskin to make their shoes!!!!!

So I tried to find a dupe, I spent weeks searching and researching and I think I found it.

IMPRESSIONS: Okay, I’m going to admit that my initial reactions were not great. The. colour was actually more off an off white as opposed to a cream colour as listed online. But I will say after wearing it and obviously taking pictures I actually like the off white colour better. I also love how soft and fluffy they are #feetpillows 💕

Quality: I have to say I wasn’t expecting these to be the highest quality considering the price and that they weren’t a brand name. But they were great, had a good sole, which when I walked around the house I couldn’t feel the floor and I didn’t slip. I also like the faux fur material it isn’t too long and it doesn’t have that micro-fiber look.

Size: I’m a 7.5, so I ordered a 7-8, its not loose at all. I would say if you have wide feet they are a narrow fit. I don’t have that problem, but I can see that being a thing

Price: The Emu slippers in comparison are around $80 CAD, hands down this is an amazing deal, I also love that they are keeping me vegan! I think if I weren’t vegan, I would actually stick with this brand, because of the price difference.

Would I Recommend: Hands down I would, and already have especially to my vegan babes that are looking for good quality vegan and stylish slippers.

P.S they have a few other colours too!
